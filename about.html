About me

My name is Ana, I'm a 23 years old designer based in London and originaly from Portugal.

I decided to move to London not only for the job opportunities the city has but also to improve my education. I'm currently taking a Masters in Graphic Design Communication to improve my design skills and knowledge, and at the same time doing some freelance work. I have experience working in Graphic Design, Web Design, Editorial Design and developing Branding and Identity. I approach tasks with a sense of professionalism and enjoy producing work that reflects my creativity and knowledge.

On a more personal note, my hobbies include reading, watching movies and TV shows, traveling, which I should do more often, walking my dog, and photography. I developed a soft spot for photography from a very young age, I would always bring my camera with me everywhere I went, and that ritual continues to this day.
